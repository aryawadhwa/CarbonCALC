## Key Features

- **Multi-User Support**: Individuals, Institutions, and Corporations
- **ML Predictions**: CPU-optimized ensemble models for forecasting
- **IoT Integration**: Real-time sensor monitoring
- **Statistical Benchmarking**: Industry comparison with percentiles
- **Free Cloud Deployment**: Runs on Render.com free tier

## Research Focus

CarbonCALC addresses the gap between carbon accounting and actionable intervention by providing:
- Integrated IoT-ML-Cloud architecture
- Accessible ML models (CPU-only, no GPU)
- Personalized quantified recommendations
- Statistical rigor in benchmarking

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python)
- **ML**: Scikit-learn (CPU-optimized)
- **Database**: SQLite/PostgreSQL
- **Frontend**: HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- **Deployment**: Free cloud hosting
